Our Story and Commitment

Eliyas Yonas

Founder & CEO

"Confidence is the only true essential."

ADISS CLOTH was founded by CEO **Eliyas Yonas** on the principle that true luxury lies in timeless design, impeccable quality, and absolute ethics. We reject fast fashion in favor of enduring style, focusing on a curated collection of essentials that are designed to last a lifetime.

Global Craftsmanship: Sourced in Ethiopia, Assembled in Europe

Our supply chain is a testament to global excellence: the luxury raw materials are ethically sourced from the rich lands of **Ethiopia**. These precious fibers are then brought to skilled, professional tailoring houses in the **UK and Italy** for meticulous assembly and finishing. This unique synergy of Ethiopian heritage and European craftsmanship is what defines our exceptional quality.
